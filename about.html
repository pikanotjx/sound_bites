SoundBites Exploring music

Are you a true music connoisseur?

SoundBites is a music trivia game that tests your knowledge of songs by popular artists. Pick your favourite artist and fight your way to be their biggest fan on the leaderboard!

How to play

SoundBites is a music trivia game that tests your knowledge of music. It is a fun and interactive way to learn more about music and the artists behind them.

Our original plan consisted of using the Spotify API to get the songs and artists. However, we were unable to get the API to work. Currently, we are using a database of songs and artists that we have manually added. We are working on getting the Spotify API to work, and we hope to have it working soon.

You can request for your favourite artist to be added by emailing us at hello@kwokjingxuan.me. However, due to the current limitations of our database, we will only be able to add artists if there is a sufficient demand for them.

We are currently working on adding more features to the game. Features we have planned for include a guess the lyrics game, multiplayer mode, and more.
